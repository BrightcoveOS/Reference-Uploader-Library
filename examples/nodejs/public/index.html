Features

  • Multi-part

    Each video is broken into many pieces and uploaded simultaneously to maximize available bandwidth and only retry failed parts -- not the entire video.

  • Direct to S3

    Don't put extra load on your servers -- upload directly to S3 saving you time and making uploads faster!

  • Configurable

    Change any text provided, easily instrument all phases of the upload and failure, tweak Evaporate.js settings directly

  • Custom Validation

    Bring your own video validation logic! Don't want 1080p? Check in the browser before you upload

  • Es6 Friendly

    Uses Webpack and Commonjs internally, but also available as a standalone UMD module.

  • Documented

    Every configuration option is documented

  • Battle Tested

    Built on top of the leading upload library, Evaporate.js
